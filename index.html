About The App

Fire District App

The FIRE DISTRICT application summarizes and filters all the data of fire incidents in the Philippines in 2012 to 2016. All Outputs are depends in the inputs that are selected or entered by the user they can use our slider to limit the range of desired number of incidents, radio buttons to choose a specific year and select box or drop down box for specific region they can also tick the check box to show data in all region and year. This inputs will result different kinds of tables and plots depends on how the user want to use it we also added a map to show the location of every Region and their populations.
